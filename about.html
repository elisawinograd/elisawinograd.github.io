Hello and welcome to my creative hub.

I am an experienced and international UX/UI designer currently based in Berlin. I love doing user research and user testing to find design solutions for products and services that need design improvement. I work with organizations and companies of all types and help them with their visual identity, user experience, and marketing.

Apart from designing, I volunteer for The Real Junk Food Project Berlin educating others, and fighting against, food waste. I am passionate about sustainability and try to live a conscious and holistic way of living.
