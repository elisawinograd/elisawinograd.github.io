Tradebay - The Global Food Trading Community

In 2021 I joined Tradebay . Tradabay is a platform created by a swedish team which helps buyers and sellers from the fruit industry navigate the marketplace without having to go through mediators. Tradebay has a public-facing website and a private app for its users. They place great enfasis on the mobile interaction of both the website and the dashboard application given that most users navigate on their mobile devices. I therefore had the opportinuty to work on scalable designs that fufilled the complicated infrastructure of the tradebay product.

Live Tradebay homepage, designed by Elisa

Defining Tradebay Branding

The first thing Tradebay wanted to define was a new branding image. I proposed a variety of color and typography variations. The final decision was made together: a bright and organic color-palette with a serious and modern look.

Restructuring of the Tradebay Website

Once we had decided on appropriate colors and fonts I applied the new look and feel to the public-facing website. We worked together on restructuring the information on the website. To do so we created a virtual site-map which you can see below.

Site-map for the Tradebay's public facing website

Design Research for the new Website

After agreeing on the information that should be on the website I gathered research and inspiration and created wireframes and flows which I presented to the team. Below are some screenshots of the presentation I shared with Tradebay in order to show the team some ideas for the website re-design.

Website Design and Development

After deciding on a layout direction I re-designed the existing website. I provided the team with a few designs to choose from. Once a design was decided upon I worked closely with the developer to create the new public website. I was given access to the code and was able to make a few UI fixes before pushing the website live. Below is a screenshot of the old public-facing website and a screenshot of the new website. Dashboard Features and Improvements

Apart from working on the UI and information arquitecture of the public facing website I helped Tradebay improve and develop new features for the dashboard. This required a lot of UX-thinking and team work.

One of the biggest campaigns I helped HYGH with was the Greentech Festival in Templehof, Berlin. In order to engage customers with the HYGH platform, they provided a sign up platform in which users could chose a screen to display their advertisements during the festival. I created the design for this flow. I based the designs on the existing styleguide we created and the styleguide provided by the Greentech Festival team. It was a challenge to combine both brands and still retain each of their unique identities. Apart from the new platform design, I designed screens for HYGH to display through out the festival as well as helped them with the imagery for social media.
