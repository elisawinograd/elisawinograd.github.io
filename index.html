Hello and welcome to my creative hub.

I am an experienced UX/UI designer and love doing research and user testing to fix problems. I also enjoy to contribute to different projects and organizations in creative ways by helping them with their branding, marketing and user satisfaction.

In my free time, I volunteer as a core team member of The Real Junk Food Project Berlin cooking and educating others to feed bellies, not bins. I am a city dweller passionate about the environment; doing my best to be active in fighting waste in general and in living a healthy and fulfilling lifestyle.
